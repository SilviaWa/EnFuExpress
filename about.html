About EnFu Express

Enfu Express started in April 2003. David Jau and His mother, Sho opened their first store in Temple Mall, Temple Tx. They serve their best and fresh everyday for their customers. They opened their second location at Abilene mall 2015. Their most popular dishes are orange chicken, sweet and sour, bell pepper and terikey chicken. Handmade cream cheese wontons and chow mein are always part of their part of their achievement. David was born in Myanmar (Burma) came to United States 2005. after finished his college degree, he ambitious to run the chinese restaurant. Under the support of family, friends and his prayer, he eventually could run a store at Temple mall. They primarily invested on customer satisfaction. When Customers come , they need fresh, delicious food and enjoyable time.
